Greenacres, FL

Greenacres is a vibrant city located in Palm Beach County, Florida, known for its motto "A Good Place to Live." Our project aims to recreate this atmosphere with high-fidelity environments that capture the unique suburban feel of the local streets. By utilizing the advanced A-Chassis system, every vehicle provides a realistic driving experience that mirrors actual Florida road conditions. We have developed custom user interfaces from scratch to ensure that players have an immersive and intuitive experience while navigating the city. This project is a dedicated tribute to the community, blending real-world geography with top-tier Roblox engineering.
